Quick start

  1. Open Teams and add at least two teams with logos & rosters.
  2. Go to Match Control, select Team A & Team B.
  3. In OBS add the overlay URLs from Browser Sources (1920×1080).
  4. Toggle overlays on/off and drive the match — OBS updates in real time.

Overlays included

  • Scorebug — top-center match bar (teams, score, side, map, BO).
  • Roster — full-screen team line-up with logos & roles.
  • Lower third — caster names/handles, shown in real time.
  • Map ban / veto — pick & ban sequence like mapban.gg.
