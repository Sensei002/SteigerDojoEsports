Tournament bracket

Group stage is double elimination (4 groups · 4 teams · top 2 advance); playoffs are single elimination (quarter → semi → final). Type team names straight into the slots, set scores, and click a name to mark the winner. Everything is manual.

Shared with the Starting Soon scene — change it in Settings → Starting Soon background.

Group stage · double elimination

Each group: two Upper Round 1 matches, an Upper Final (decides the 1st seed), a Lower Round 1, and a Lower Final (decides the 2nd seed).

Playoffs · single elimination

Eight qualifiers: four quarter-finals, two semi-finals, one final.
